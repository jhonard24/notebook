About Barangay Longos

Barangay Longos is one of the vibrant communities in Malabon City, known for its rich culture, strong community spirit, and commitment to progress. Our barangay is dedicated to serving our residents with integrity and compassion.

With a population of approximately 15,000 residents, we maintain a clean, peaceful, and business-friendly environment that fosters growth and development.

City of Malabon
